Day 1 Reminder

It was narrated that Abu Hurairah said:
"The messenger of Allah said: There has come to you Ramadan,a blessed month, which Allah, the Mighty and Sublime, has enjoined you to fast. In it the gates of heavens are opened and the gates of hell are closed, and every devil is chained up. In it Allah has a night which is better than a thousand months; whoever is deprived of it's goodness is indeed deprived."
[Sunan an-Nasa'i]
